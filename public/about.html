ABOUT

Strawberry Skies is a genre-bending band blending dreamy melodies with raw, heartfelt lyrics. Drawing inspiration from indie rock, alt-pop, and a touch of synth, their sound takes listeners on a journey through ethereal soundscapes and honest emotion. Whether you're floating along to their mellow tunes or caught in the energy of their more upbeat tracks, Strawberry Skies creates music that feels both nostalgic and refreshingly new. The band is all about crafting authentic, immersive experiences, inviting listeners to find meaning in the beauty of everyday moments.
